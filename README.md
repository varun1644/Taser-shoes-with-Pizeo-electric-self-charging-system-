# **Hyper Shoes – Smart Women Safety Device**

## **Overview**

Hyper Shoes is a smart wearable safety system designed to enhance women’s safety using embedded electronics, self-defense mechanisms, wireless security concepts, and energy harvesting technologies. The project integrates multiple safety-oriented functionalities into a footwear-based platform to provide portable and easily accessible protection during emergency situations. 

The system combines:

* Self-defense technology
* Wireless surveillance protection
* Self-powering energy system
* Embedded mechatronics integration

# **Project Objective**

The objective of the project is to develop a smart shoe capable of:

* Providing emergency self-defense support
* Detecting and preventing unauthorized Wi-Fi camera surveillance
* Generating electrical energy through walking motion
* Integrating safety technologies into wearable systems

The project focuses on improving women’s safety using compact embedded technologies and portable automation systems. 

---

# **Key Features**

## **1. Self Defense System**

The Hyper Shoes include an electro-shock-based self-defense mechanism designed for emergency protection situations.

The system delivers:

* High voltage
* Low current pulses
* Temporary neuromuscular incapacitation

The objective is to temporarily disable an attacker while minimizing long-term physical harm. 

### **Working Principle**

* A compact high-voltage module is integrated into the shoe
* Trigger activation initiates the electroshock system
* The pulse creates temporary muscular disruption

---

## **2. Wi-Fi Camera Protection System**

The project proposes a Wi-Fi camera protocol/jamming concept intended to address unauthorized hidden Wi-Fi surveillance cameras in:

* Hotel rooms
* Trial rooms
* Public restrooms
* Private spaces

The concept aims to integrate a mobile-controlled wireless protection system to detect or interfere with unauthorized Wi-Fi camera activity. 

### **Purpose**

* Enhance privacy protection
* Improve awareness against hidden surveillance
* Provide additional security for women in public/private spaces

---

## **3. Self Charging System**

The Hyper Shoes integrate a piezoelectric energy harvesting mechanism that converts walking pressure into electrical energy. 

### **Technology Used**

* Piezoelectric plates
* Mechanical stress energy conversion
* Embedded low-power charging system

### **Working Principle**

* Walking pressure applies force on piezoelectric plates
* Mechanical stress generates electrical charge
* Generated power can support low-power electronic modules
